Engr. Dr. John Lander Ichenwo is a visionary leader with over two decades of expertise spanning academia and the energy industry, Dr. Ichenwo holds a PhD in Petroleum Engineering from Rivers State University, specializing in Well Operations. His distinguished career includes pivotal roles at global energy giants such as Shell, Weatherford, and Baker Hughes, where he advanced drilling technology, safety protocols, and well optimization strategies.

As a seasoned senior lecturer and consultant, Dr. Ichenwo merges theoretical rigor with practical innovation, having mentored future engineers and championed cutting-edge approaches in petroleum engineering.

Driven by a mission to empower communities, he founded the John Lander Energy Institute to bridge the gap between academia and industry, fostering skills in energy, oil, and gas sectors. His journey, marked by resilience and dedication exemplifies turning challenges into pathways for success, inspiring professionals to unlock their highest potential.
